//craetional design pattern

Factory method :
follows two of the SOLID principle -> 
Single responsibility method (class should only have one reason to change)
Open/closed principle: open for extension but closed for modification.

4 things are there:
1) Creator interface : VehicleFactory
2) concrete Creator : TwoWheelerFactor, FourWheelerFactor
3) Producer interface : Vehicle
4) Concrete Products : TwoWheeler, FourWheeler
